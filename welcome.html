• Shotokan Karate: a style of karate emphasizing deep stances and powerful strikes, founded by Gichin Funakoshi in Okinawa, Japan.
  • Japanese Ju-Jitsu: a grappling art, incorporates escaping from holds, chokes, grabs, as well as throwing, joint bending, and choking techniques as a means of dispatching an attacker.
  • Ryukyu Kobudo: traditional Okinawan weapon training, with all weapons having originally been implemented from common farm tools.
